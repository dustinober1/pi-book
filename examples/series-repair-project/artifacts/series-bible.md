# Series Bible

## Series identity

- series title: The Hollow Port Files
- genre / subgenre: speculative thriller
- core promise: each book reveals a larger civic conspiracy while deepening character costs
- tone and voice continuity: atmospheric but unsentimental, quick dialogue, precise physical detail
- series status: mixed

## Canon status

- locked canon installments: Book 1
- editable installments: Books 2-4
- unpublished/planned installments: Book 5
- non-negotiable published facts: Mara exposed the ferry archive fire cover-up in Book 1
- allowed retcon scope: clarification only; no contradiction of published events
