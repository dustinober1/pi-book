# Brief

## Original idea

Repair an existing series where Book 1 is already published and fixed, while later books need continuity verification and selective rewrites.

## Intake scaffold

- Language: English
- Genre: speculative thriller
- Audience: adult crossover series readers
- Target length: 80,000-95,000 words per editable installment
- Narrative mode: close third-person multi-POV
- Workflow mode: series repair
- Reader promise: each later book should feel fully aligned with the published first installment while escalating series stakes
- Canon status: Book 1 locked canon; Books 2-4 editable after verification
