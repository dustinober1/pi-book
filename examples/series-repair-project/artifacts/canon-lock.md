# Canon Lock

## Locked source of truth

- locked installment(s): Book 1
- edition / source used: final published paperback
- publication status: published
- why this canon is immutable: already in market; reader trust depends on preserving established facts

## Immutable constraints

- character facts that cannot change: Mara is a municipal archivist turned whistleblower
- relationship facts that cannot change: Mara and Jonas end Book 1 estranged but still allied against the city manager
- world rules that cannot change: the harbor anomaly is real but poorly understood, not openly public knowledge
- chronology anchors that cannot change: Book 2 begins within six weeks of Book 1
- revealed secrets / twists that cannot be undone: the archive fire was deliberate
- promises later books must pay off: who funded the cover-up and why the anomaly matters

## Flexible interpretation zone

- facts that are implied but not explicit: how many officials knew the full truth
- ambiguities that later books may reinterpret safely: Jonas's off-page contacts
- continuity gaps that can be clarified without retconning canon: exact timeline of pre-Book-1 research
