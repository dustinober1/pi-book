# Series Repair Service

Use this when a writer has an existing series with at least one locked book and one or more later books that may be revised.

## Best-fit cases

- Book 1 is published and cannot change
- later books drift from established canon
- character voice or relationship logic changed across installments
- later books repeat structure, under-escalate, or break promises from earlier books
- the writer wants verification before deciding between light revision and full rewrite

## Core promise

Preserve locked canon, diagnose what broke in later installments, and create a clean repair path without forcing the whole series to restart from zero.

## Recommended offer structure

### 1. Canon Extraction

Deliverables:

- `artifacts/series-bible.md`
- `artifacts/series-timeline.md`
- `artifacts/character-state-matrix.md`
- `artifacts/reveal-spoiler-matrix.md`
- `artifacts/canon-lock.md`
- `artifacts/installment-promise-tracker.md`
- `artifacts/series-payoff-ledger.md`

Outcome:

- the published or locked installment becomes durable source-of-truth documentation

### 2. Series Verification Audit

Deliverables:

- `artifacts/series-verification-matrix.md`
- `artifacts/series-payoff-ledger.md`
- `artifacts/retcon-log.md`
- `artifacts/series-repetition-radar.md`
- `artifacts/continuity-ledger.md`
- `artifacts/revision-tickets.md`
- `evaluations/series-regression-check.md`

Outcome:

- each editable book gets a pass/fail-style verification view across canon, voice, escalation, and promise alignment

### 3. Rewrite Strategy

Deliverables:

- repaired or rebuilt `artifacts/05-outline.md`
- updated `artifacts/05-subplot-map.md`
- updated `artifacts/reader-promise-tracker.md`
- per-book `artifacts/book-handoff-packet.md` where needed
- rewrite plan in `artifacts/revision-tickets.md`

Outcome:

- the writer can choose surgical revision, partial rebuild, or full rewrite with evidence

### 4. Installment Rewrite

Deliverables:

- rewritten `manuscript/chapters/`
- updated scorecards and audit files

Outcome:

- repaired installment that fits both the locked book and the future series plan

## Suggested client language

- **Series Verification**: audit existing books against published canon, continuity, voice, and payoff obligations
- **Series Repair**: fix later installments without breaking the published foundation
- **Locked-Canon Rewrite Planning**: reverse-engineer Book 1, then rebuild Books 2+ safely

## Simple engagement tiers

### Audit only

Good for:

- writers who want diagnosis before rewriting

Deliver:

- canon extraction
- verification matrix
- revision tickets

### Audit + rewrite plan

Good for:

- writers who need architecture before drafting again

Deliver:

- audit package
- rebuilt outline
- subplot and promise repair plan

### Full repair

Good for:

- writers who want later books revised or rewritten

Deliver:

- audit package
- rewrite plan
- revised manuscript files
- adversarial audit and score

## Scope guardrails

- locked canon is not silently changed
- any retcon must be explicit and approved
- later-book improvements do not count if they break Book 1 logic
- voice cleanup should preserve author fingerprint, not genericize it
- not every issue requires a full rewrite; use revision tickets first

## Good discovery questions

- Which books are already published or otherwise locked?
- Which books are editable?
- Is the goal verification, light revision, or full rewrite?
- Are there known continuity breaks already bothering readers?
- Which character, relationship, or world elements are non-negotiable?
- Do later books need to align only with Book 1, or also with planned future books?
