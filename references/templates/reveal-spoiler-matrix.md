# Reveal / Spoiler Matrix

## Purpose

Track what is true, when it is seeded, when it is revealed, and who knows it at each stage so the series does not accidentally repeat, spoil, or forget key information.

## Forbidden reveal errors

- accidental re-reveal as if new information
- impossible ignorance after an explicit earlier reveal
- later-book spoilage of an earlier-book mystery without intent
- characters acting on knowledge they do not yet have
