## Required promise types to consider

- premise promise
- genre/use-case promise
- emotional promise
- mystery, argument, learning, or suspense promise

## Payoff strength guide

Use `decisive`, `transformed`, `deliberately denied`, `series-deferred with one battle closed`, or `accepted risk`. Do not mark a major promise complete with mere containment, thematic residue, or a private realization unless `publication-shape.md` explicitly accepts that risk.
