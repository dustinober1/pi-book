# Character Humility Guardrail

## Purpose

Keep biblical characters human without reducing them to modern psychology, cheap motive, trauma formula, romance machinery, ambition, resentment, or author argument.

## Humility principles

- Humanize without trivializing.
- Leave some motives partly mysterious when the source is silent.
- Translate inner life into action, obligation, kinship, ritual, work, danger, shame, hope, obedience, fear, and silence.
- Do not make every character a therapy case or a modern rebel.
- Do not make faith, doubt, sin, repentance, or obedience into a single neat explanation.
