## Ending gate

- if standalone: require at least one irreversible external consequence
- if series opener: signal that one battle closed and a larger conflict remains
- containment alone is not enough; pair containment with sacrifice, exposure, publication, public testimony, legal record, destroyed defense, or institutional fracture
- moral ambiguity may remain, but the protagonist must make a decisive choice that changes the record or the world

## Risk note

- if the ending is intellectually honest but emotionally or commercially under-resolved, revise for publication shape rather than only theme
