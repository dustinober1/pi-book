## Reader clarity rules

- seed system limits before violations
- seed emergency exceptions before they become climax-critical
- if a new surface appears late, it must have earlier setup or be treated as a revision risk
- the reader should understand what the system can touch directly, what it can only suggest, and what requires delegated trust
