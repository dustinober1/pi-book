# Anachronism / Modernity Audit

## Purpose

Catch modern assumptions, idioms, psychology, politics, romance beats, church habits, and values imported into ancient settings without intention.

## Register / bureaucracy watchlist

Modern project-management, administrative, and technocratic terms that slip into ancient, historical, or sacred-retelling prose and break immersion. Some may be **intentional** (e.g., an antagonist whose bureaucracy is part of the critique); mark those `intentional contrast` and keep them confined to that voice. The rest should be translated into period-plausible language.

## Repair principles

- Translate modern emotional realities into period-plausible social pressure, action, silence, obligation, shame, honor, kinship, ritual, work, money, or danger.
- Do not erase human interiority; make it historically and culturally embodied.
