## Sacred-figure checks

- Does portrayal contradict explicit source?
- Does the book over-explain what should remain holy, strange, or mysterious?
- Does humanizing become trivializing?
- Does reverence become flattening?
- Does villain complexity become excuse-making?
- Does the figure sound like a modern protagonist in costume?
