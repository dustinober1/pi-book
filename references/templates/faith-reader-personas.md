# Faith Reader Personas

## Purpose

Create a sacred-retelling-specific reader panel so feedback can distinguish true reader-trust risks from wrong-reader noise.

## Persona panel

| persona | what they need | what they distrust | likely objection | what would win trust | priority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Devotional reader | reverence and spiritual resonance | cleverness that feels cold | pending | pending | pending |
| Biblical-literalist reader | source fidelity and clear invention boundary | invented material presented as fact | pending | pending | pending |
| Historical-context reader | ancient-world credibility | modern assumptions in costume | pending | pending | pending |
| Literary fiction reader | character complexity and language | sermon disguised as fiction | pending | pending | pending |
| Skeptical but respectful reader | honesty and restraint | manipulation or certainty inflation | pending | pending | pending |
