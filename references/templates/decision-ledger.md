# Decision Ledger

Track approved pivots, rejected ideas, unresolved choices, and decisions automation must not silently reverse.

| Date | Decision | Source / evidence | Status | Do-not-reintroduce notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Required entries

Record decisions about:

- premise changes
- audience/market positioning
- voice changes
- ending changes
- character deaths or major relationship outcomes
- POV changes
- genre promise changes
- risk/controversy reduction
- major structural pivots
- rejected openings, endings, names, tones, and plot turns
