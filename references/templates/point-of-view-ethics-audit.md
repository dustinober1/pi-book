## High-risk checks

- Does the POV contradict explicit scripture?
- Does the POV turn mystery into over-explained psychology?
- Does the POV make a sacred figure serve modern therapy language or author argument?
- Does the POV make a villain, doubter, woman, outsider, or disciple simpler than the source allows?
- Does the POV preserve awe, strangeness, and limitation where appropriate?
