# Sacred Residue Audit

## Purpose

Check whether the ending and major turns leave the right spiritual, emotional, and moral aftertaste for a sacred retelling. Plot closure is not enough; the reader should leave with the intended reverence, awe, grief, repentance, hope, holy discomfort, or mystery.

## Final check

- Does the story end as a lived encounter rather than a lesson summary?
- Does it preserve negative capability where the source is mysterious?
- Does it invite reflection without manipulating devotion?
- Does the final Author's Note support rather than flatten the residue?
