# Ticketed Revision

Apply one ticket or a coherent ticket group. Protect canon, reveal order, voice, and unaffected work. Close only after acceptance and regression checks pass.

For tightening passes, remove scaffolding before polishing style. Cut repeated thematic explanation after the scene has already made the point, vary paragraph endings away from aphoristic closure, and preserve only the dialogue lines that belong to the speaker's age, role, and immediate emotional need.

For procedural, legal, technical, medical, historical, or other domain-dependent scenes, repair the institutional posture and admissibility/access mechanics before line edits. A scene may stay limited or ambiguous, but the ambiguity must be chosen by the story rather than caused by missing logistics.
