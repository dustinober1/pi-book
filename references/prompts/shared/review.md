# Milestone Review

Run independent review lanes, cite evidence, separate blockers from preference, synthesize revision tickets, and require regression checks after repair.

Compare the manuscript with `remarkability.yaml`, but distinguish planned ambition from achieved impact. Treat only recorded real-reader responses in `reader-experiments.yaml` as outside-reader evidence; personas and models may prepare questions but cannot validate recall, recommendation, or talkability.

Escalate as blockers any retained drafting scaffolding, metafictional chapter/book references that are not an explicit formal strategy, continuity errors in chronology or identity facts, and genre-domain plausibility gaps that would break trust for an informed reader.

When assessing theme, prefer scene evidence over stated intent: flag repeated summations, quotable aphorism density, and character dialogue that sounds like the manuscript's thesis rather than the character's private pressure. Require at least one concrete dramatized counter-case before accepting a public-good or opposition argument that is otherwise carried by speeches or statistics.
