# Chapter Drafting

Draft one approved chapter packet using only relevant context. Make every chapter alter pressure, state, relationship, evidence, reader forecast, or the path to the ending. Use the compact remarkability contract to protect the book's hook, signature moments, productive disagreement, and residue without mechanically restating them or manufacturing quotable lines. Update compact state after the chapter; do not run the full review stack.

Keep craft intent out of the prose: no references to the chapter, book, scene function, story requirement, or planned movement unless the approved form is overt metafiction. Let objects, choices, dialogue, and procedural friction carry the theme before adding any interpretive sentence.
