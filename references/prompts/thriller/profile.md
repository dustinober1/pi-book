# Thriller Profile

Track threat, evidence provenance, reader forecast, protagonist choice, opposition logic, procedural plausibility, midpoint state change, and climax legibility.

For legal, institutional, or technical thrillers, make the posture legible early: who has authority, who moves, what evidence can be inspected, and what limits bind each actor. Opposition logic must be dramatized through at least one concrete case, cost, or beneficiary, not only through abstract defense. Vulnerabilities should require effort proportionate to the system's sophistication.
