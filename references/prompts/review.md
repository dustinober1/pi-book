# Thriller Review Prompt

Use the `thriller-forge-for-pi` skill.

Review the active book or series for thriller-series quality only.

Check:

- repeated scene engines
- weak middle movement
- passive lead character
- reveals that do not change reader forecast
- clue and payoff drift
- unclear ending logic
- characters with only one function
- overexplained themes
- continuity drift
- untracked series promises

Write findings into the relevant audit file and convert concrete problems into `revision-tickets.md`.
