# Thriller Series Planning Prompt

Use the `thriller-forge-for-pi` skill.

## Goal

Create or repair the series-level plan for a commercial thriller series.

## Read first

- `SERIES_STATE.yaml`
- `series/series-bible.md`
- `series/series-arc.md`
- `series/cast-and-pressure-map.md`
- `series/reveal-ledger.md`
- `series/continuity-ledger.md`
- `series/market-package.md`

## Required outputs

Update only these files unless the user explicitly asks otherwise:

- `series/series-bible.md`
- `series/series-arc.md`
- `series/cast-and-pressure-map.md`
- `series/reveal-ledger.md`
- `series/continuity-ledger.md`
- `series/market-package.md`
- `STATUS.md`

## Series standards

The series plan must define:

- the recurring protagonist or lead cast
- the core commercial hook
- the recurring pressure pattern
- the book-to-book escalation model
- the reader read-through promise
- the long secret, unresolved wound, enemy, institution, or mystery that can sustain multiple books
- what closes in each book and what remains open for the next book

## Keep it lean

Do not over-plan every future book at scene level. Track future books as promises, pressure, escalation, and locked constraints only.
