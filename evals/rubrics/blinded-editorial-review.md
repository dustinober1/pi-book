Score each dimension from 1–10 and cite textual evidence:

1. Author-specific voice and sentence behavior
2. Character differentiation under pressure
3. Therefore/but causal movement
4. Thriller suspense or romantasy emotional/fantasy promise
5. Scene-engine specificity and embodiment
6. Subtext and omission
7. Continuity and setup/payoff integrity
8. Ending pressure and next-page pull
