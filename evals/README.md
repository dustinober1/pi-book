# Novel Forge evaluations

The automated fixtures test genre configuration, chapter packets, act architecture, sample length, review-ticket synthesis, and regression protections for thriller and romantasy, standalone and series projects.

They do not claim to prove literary excellence. Use `rubrics/blinded-editorial-review.md` for matched prose comparisons before a major release. Remove workflow labels, randomize samples, and have fresh reviewers score voice specificity, causal movement, character differentiation, genre promise, continuity, subtext, and reader pull.
