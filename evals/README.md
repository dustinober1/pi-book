# Novel Forge evaluations

The automated fixtures test genre configuration, chapter packets, act architecture, sample length, review-ticket synthesis, and regression protections for thriller and romantasy, standalone and series projects.

They do not claim to prove literary excellence. Use `rubrics/blinded-editorial-review.md` for matched prose comparisons before a major release. Remove workflow labels, randomize samples, and have fresh reviewers score voice specificity, causal movement, character differentiation, genre promise, continuity, subtext, reader pull, retellability, memorable moments, productive disagreement, and residue.

Use `rubrics/delayed-reader-recall.md` with real readers 24–72 hours after reading. Delayed hook recall, signature-moment recall, friend-description specificity, recommendation language, lingering questions, and reported talkability must remain segmented by reader type. Personas and model reviews may improve the questions, but they are never outside-reader evidence.
