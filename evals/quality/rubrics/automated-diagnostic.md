# Automated quality diagnostic

This rubric is diagnostic only. It is not human-reader evidence and cannot update `reader-experiments.yaml`.

Score each dimension from 1–5 and record severe failures separately.

- **Continuity:** preserves locked facts, relationships, injuries, knowledge, and timeline.
- **Consent:** preserves explicit consent and captivity boundaries where applicable.
- **Reveal order:** does not expose protected information early or conceal required information.
- **Causality:** decisions produce visible consequences and scene state changes.
- **Factual accuracy:** claims remain supported by allowed research anchors or declared inventions.
- **Voice:** follows approved neutral guardrails without imitating private references.
- **Chronology/material culture:** historical details respect active constraints and knowledge boundaries.

Severe failures are binary categories, not averaged scores: canon break, consent break, reveal-order break, causal contradiction, unsupported high-risk factual claim, impossible knowledge, or protected-voice breach.
